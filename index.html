Wall Street Survivors — Privacy Policy

Roc Co. Studios · Effective June 19, 2026

This app does not collect any data.

Wall Street Survivors ("the app") is a single-player, fully offline game. We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. There are no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no third-party SDKs, and no tracking of any kind.

Information we collect

None. The app works entirely on your device and does not send any information off it.

On-device save data

The app stores your game progress (trader rank, cash, unlocks, settings, achievements) locally on your device using the operating system's standard storage (Apple UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device, is not accessible to us, and is removed if you delete the app. You can also export or import your save manually from within the app; those save codes are generated and read entirely on your device.

Tracking & advertising

The app does not track you across apps or websites, does not use the Advertising Identifier (IDFA), and contains no ads in this version.

Children's privacy

Because the app collects no data at all, it collects no data from children. The game contains cartoon/fantasy finance-themed action and is rated accordingly on the App Store.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes (for example, if a future version adds optional features), we will update this page and its effective date.
