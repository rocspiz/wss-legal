Wall Street Survivors — Privacy Policy

Roc Co. Studios · Effective June 20, 2026

No accounts. No name or email. We don't track you across other apps.

Wall Street Survivors ("the app," "we," "us") is a single-player game by Roc Co. Studios. We do not require an account and we do not ask for personal details like your name or email. To show ads and to offer one optional purchase, the app uses two third-party services that collect limited technical and advertising-related data, described below. None of this data is linked to your real-world identity, and none of it is used to track you across other companies' apps or websites.

Information collected

We do not collect personal identity information ourselves. The following data is collected by our third-party partners so that ads and purchases can function:

Advertising — Google AdMob. To display ads, Google's Mobile Ads SDK may collect:

  • a device / advertising identifier;
  • approximate ("coarse") location estimated from your IP address;
  • advertising data (which ads were shown) and in-app interactions such as taps and video views;
  • crash logs and performance data (e.g., launch time, energy use) to keep the SDK working.

The app serves non-personalized (contextual) ads only. It does not display an App Tracking Transparency prompt and does not use this data to track you across other apps or websites.

Purchases — RevenueCat & Apple. If you buy the optional "Insider Pass," the transaction is processed by Apple. We use RevenueCat to manage and restore purchases; it records your purchase history and an anonymous identifier so the purchase can be restored on your devices. Your payment details (card number, etc.) are handled entirely by Apple — we never see or receive them.

On-device save data

The app stores your game progress (trader rank, cash, unlocks, settings, achievements) locally on your device using the operating system's standard storage (Apple UserDefaults). This data stays on your device, is not accessible to us, and is removed if you delete the app. You can also export or import your save manually from within the app; those save codes are generated and read entirely on your device.

Tracking

We do not track you. We do not link your data with third-party data for targeted advertising or advertising measurement, and we do not share data with data brokers. Ads are non-personalized, and we do not request access to the Advertising Identifier (IDFA) via App Tracking Transparency.

Your choices

  • Remove ads: the one-time "Insider Pass" in-app purchase removes all ads.
  • Disable ad offers: a toggle in the app's Settings turns off the optional "watch an ad" bonus offers.
  • Advertising identifier: you can limit or reset it in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security.

Third-party services

These partners process the data above under their own privacy policies:

  • Google (AdMob): policies.google.com/privacy
  • RevenueCat: revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Apple (App Store / purchases): apple.com/legal/privacy

Children's privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13 and is rated 13+ on the App Store. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13, and the ads shown are non-personalized.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and its effective date.
